# Module 07 Discussion 1 - How Should a Shopping App be Organized?

## Purpose
Reflect on how widely used shopping apps organize sites effectively to enable users to understand if it is possible to do what they'd like to do and help users to find what they're looking for.

## Prompt

**Initial post (250 words max):**
Consider a shopping site you've used recently. Describe two ways in which the shopping site helped you to find what you were looking for.

**Reply post (100 words min):**
Reflect on how the site helped. What was the key principle that the site used to enable this? To what extent is this site reflected more broadly across all shopping sites?
