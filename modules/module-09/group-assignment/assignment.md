# Module 9 Group Assignment: Interaction Design Critique and Iteration

**Due:** Sunday, March 23, 2026 at 11:59 PM
**Points:** 100
**Submit:** Document via Canvas (one per group)

---

## Purpose

Critique the interaction design of your app based on the principles in Modules 7, 8, and 9 to identify 7 interaction design weaknesses and then revise your interaction design and app to address these weaknesses.

## Instructions

1. Provide separate URLs for:
   - The previous version of your web app
   - The new, updated version of your web app

2. Critique the previous version using principles and guidelines from **Site Design**, **Interaction Techniques**, and **Preventing Error** lectures and readings, identifying **7 weaknesses**. For each weakness:
   1. Representative screenshot of the issue
   2. One paragraph explanation referencing the underlying principle, guideline, or theory

3. Across the 7 weaknesses, use at least **5 distinct** principles, guidelines, and theories.

4. Address each weakness by revising the interaction design. For each:
   1. Representative screenshot of the new design
   2. Short paragraph describing the change and how it addresses the issue

## Grading Criteria

1. Degree to which identified weaknesses follow from interaction design principles, guidelines, and theories
2. Effectiveness with which the changes address the identified issues

## Deliverable

**Document containing:**
- Group member names, project name, old and new URLs
- For each of the 7 issues: problem description, new design description, and before/after screenshots

**Statement of Contributions** (submitted separately by each group member):
- Your name, list of group members
- One or two paragraph statement describing how each member contributed
