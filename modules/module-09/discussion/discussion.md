# Module 9 Discussion: Unsafe Act

**Due:** Thursday, March 19, 2026 at 11:59 PM
**Points:** 100

---

## Purpose

Reflect on what causes error in your everyday interactions with software.

## Initial Post Prompt (250 words max)

Think of the last unsafe act you performed in a piece of software, where you performed an unsafe act, either an unintended slip or lapse or intentionally did something you know you shouldn't.

Describe the situation, including what you did. What do you think might have been the underlying cause for the action that you took?

## Reply Post Prompt (100 words min)

Reflect further on the situation described. What other causes might have led to the unsafe act that might have occurred?

## Deliverable

- Initial post (250 words maximum)
- Respond to at least one peer post (minimum 100 words)
